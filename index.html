Fostering Bridges Between Ukraine and India

Bharat Maidan is a civic initiative dedicated to strengthening cultural, educational, business and municipal cooperation between Ukraine and India.

Find Out More

Who We Are

Established in 2024, NGO “Bharat Maidan” brings together experts in Ukraine–India relations and members of the Indian diaspora in Ukraine. We are passionate about strengthening cultural, educational, business and municipal cooperation. For us, this mission is not just work — it is a meaningful part of our lives.
