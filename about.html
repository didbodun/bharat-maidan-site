Who We Are

Founded in 2024, Bharat Maidan is a Ukrainian NGO bringing together experts in India–Ukraine relations, members of the Indian diaspora, and professionals in diplomacy, business and academia.

We operate at the intersection of public diplomacy, economic cooperation and cultural exchange, aiming to build resilient institutional ties between both nations.

Mission

To create sustainable mechanisms of cooperation between Ukraine and India through strategic dialogue, policy engagement, municipal partnerships and business facilitation.

Vision

A resilient, future-oriented Ukraine–India partnership built on shared democratic values, innovation and long-term trust.
