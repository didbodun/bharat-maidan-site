Who We Are

Founded in 2024, Bharat Maidan is a Ukrainian NGO dedicated to strengthening relations between Ukraine and India. We bring together experts in diplomacy, business, academia and public initiatives, as well as members of the Indian diaspora in Ukraine. Our goal is to create long-term cooperation through public diplomacy, municipal partnerships, academic exchange and cultural dialogue.
