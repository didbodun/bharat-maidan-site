Introduction

The first grand Indian beauty and dance competition in history will take place in Kyiv on June 6 and 7, 2026.

The “Maharani” beauty and dance competition is an international premium platform that celebrates confidence, individuality, and talent.

About the event

We invite future beauty and dance queens to step forward and claim their place on a stage where elegance meets strength.

Only 100 participants in the beauty competition and 60 in the dance competition.

You can expect

  • International jury panel
  • Professional sound and lighting
  • Exclusive awards
  • Many surprises and vivid impressions

This grand initiative aims to build a cultural bridge between India and Ukraine, offering a powerful platform for women to showcase their beauty, talent, and skills.

Our Partners & Expertise

Bharat Maidan is a Ukrainian NGO dedicated to advancing strategic relations between Ukraine and India. Our mission is to create meaningful connections between the two countries through dialogue, cooperation, and long-term partnership initiatives.

Our strength lies in our network. We collaborate with representatives of government institutions, members of parliament, business leaders, academic experts, cultural actors, and members of the Indian diaspora in Ukraine.

Our team brings together practical experience in government affairs, international cooperation, business development, and cultural diplomacy. This combination allows us to understand both institutional processes and the practical needs of cross-border collaboration.

Through public diplomacy, municipal cooperation, academic exchange, and institutional engagement, we create structured opportunities for sustainable Ukraine–India collaboration.
