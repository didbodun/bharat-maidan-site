Who We Are

Founded in 2024, Bharat Maidan is a Ukrainian NGO bringing together experts in India–Ukraine relations, members of the Indian diaspora, and professionals in diplomacy, business and academia.

Mission

To build sustainable bridges between India and Ukraine through strategic dialogue, cultural exchange and economic cooperation.

Vision

A strong, long-term partnership between India and Ukraine based on trust, innovation and shared democratic values.

What We Do

  • Public diplomacy initiatives
  • Municipal partnerships
  • Business cooperation facilitation
  • Academic and youth exchange programs
  • Cultural dialogue projects
