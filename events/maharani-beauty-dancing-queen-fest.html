Maharani Beauty & Dancing Queen Fest

The first grand Indian beauty and dance pageant in history is designed to celebrate talent, confidence, and culture while connecting audiences across Ukraine and India.

The festival combines a pageant stage, dance performances, and cross-cultural presentations in one landmark event format. It highlights creativity, heritage, and modern expression in a single platform for participants and visitors.

Event dates: 06.06.2026 - 07.06.2025
