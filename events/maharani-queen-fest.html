MAHARANI THE QUEEN

Culture | 23.04.26

The first grand Indian beauty and dance competition in history will take place in Kyiv on June 6 and 7, 2026.

The “Maharani” beauty and dance competition is an international premium platform that celebrates confidence, individuality, and talent.

We invite future beauty and dance queens to step forward and claim their place on a stage where elegance meets strength.

Only 100 participants in the beauty competition and 60 in the dance competition.

You can expect:
International jury panel 🌏
Professional sound and lighting 📸
Exclusive awards 👑
Many surprises and vivid impressions ✨

This grand initiative aims to build a cultural bridge between India and Ukraine, offering a powerful platform for women to showcase their beauty, talent, and skills.

Registration is open.
