Who Are Bharat Maidan

Established in 2024, NGO Bharat Maidan began with a clear mission — to unite Indian business owners in Ukraine. Since then, it has evolved into a dynamic platform for India–Ukraine cooperation, connecting people, institutions, and opportunities across borders.

Bharat Maidan is led by a team of experienced and passionate professionals in linguistics, economics, and diplomacy, delivering comprehensive 360-degree strategies tailored for business leaders, government representatives, tourists, and students.

Our Experience

Over the years, we have built strong and trusted relationships with the Embassy of India in Ukraine, the Embassy of Ukraine in India, regional and municipal authorities, and the Indian business community in Ukraine.

We have actively contributed to intercity partnership strategies in Kyiv, Vinnytsia, Lviv, and Ivano-Frankivsk, supporting long-term and sustainable collaboration.

  • Supporting companies in adapting to Ukrainian legal and regulatory frameworks.
  • Providing business consulting across tourism, hospitality, and entrepreneurship.
  • Developing strategies from market entry points to full-scale development plans.
  • Conducting cross-sector analysis for strategic decision-making.

Our Values

Quality: We deliver impactful, practical, and sustainable solutions.

Collaboration: We work closely with partners and institutions to produce long-term outcomes.

Innovation: We build modern tools, including digital platforms and interactive solutions, to strengthen India–Ukraine cooperation.

Conclusion

Today, Bharat Maidan operates across Ukraine, India, and Poland — offering expertise, cultural initiatives, and strategic solutions that create real opportunities between nations.

If it’s India–Ukraine, it’s us.
