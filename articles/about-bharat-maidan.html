About Bharat Maidan

News & Events | 2026-03-08

Who Are Bharat Maidan

Established in 2024, NGO Bharat Maidan began with a clear mission — to unite Indian business owners in Ukraine. Since then, it has evolved into a dynamic platform for India–Ukraine cooperation, connecting people, institutions, and opportunities across borders.

Bharat Maidan is led by a team of experienced and passionate professionals in linguistics, economics, and diplomacy, delivering comprehensive 360-degree strategies tailored for business leaders, government representatives, tourists, and students.

Our Experience

Over the years, we have built strong and trusted relationships with:

  • The Embassy of India in Ukraine
  • The Embassy of Ukraine in India
  • Regional and municipal authorities
  • The Indian business community in Ukraine

We have actively contributed to intercity partnership strategies, identifying key entry points for cooperation in:

  • Kyiv
  • Vinnytsia
  • Lviv
  • Ivano-Frankivsk

These initiatives support long-term, sustainable collaboration between regions and institutions.

Our work also includes:

  1. Supporting companies in adapting to Ukrainian legal and regulatory frameworks.
  2. Providing business consulting across sectors such as tourism, hospitality, and local entrepreneurship.
  3. Developing strategies — from market entry points to full-scale development plans.
  4. Conducting cross-sector analysis to guide informed and strategic decision-making.

Our Values

At Bharat Maidan, our work is guided by a strong foundation of principles that shape every initiative we undertake.

Quality

We are committed to delivering impactful, well-structured solutions that are reliable, sustainable, and tailored to real-world needs — maintaining a consistently high standard in everything we do.

Collaboration

We work closely with partners, institutions, and communities to understand their goals and challenges, building meaningful cooperation that leads to practical and lasting outcomes.

Innovation

We develop modern tools — including interactive maps and digital platforms — to strengthen India–Ukraine cooperation, driven by the belief that our nations can achieve greater results together through innovation and shared vision.

Conclusion

Today, Bharat Maidan operates across Ukraine, India, and Poland — offering expertise, cultural initiatives, and strategic solutions that strengthen connections and create real opportunities between nations.

If it’s India–Ukraine, it’s us.

FAQ

What does Bharat Maidan do?

Bharat Maidan focuses on building cooperation between Ukraine and India through business consulting, cultural initiatives, and institutional partnerships.

Who can work with Bharat Maidan?

We collaborate with business owners, government representatives, students, tourists, and organizations interested in India–Ukraine relations.

How does Bharat Maidan support businesses?

We help businesses enter new markets, adapt to local regulations, and develop strategies for sustainable growth and cross-border cooperation.

In which countries does Bharat Maidan operate?

Our team and activities are currently based in Ukraine, India, and Poland, allowing us to support international cooperation effectively.
